Customer Support Ticket Resolution Knowledge Base

This document contains summarized resolutions from previously closed customer support tickets.
It helps support agents respond faster and more accurately to recurring issues.

Login and Authentication Issues:
Several customers reported inability to log into their accounts.
Most cases were resolved by resetting passwords and clearing browser cache.
In some cases, account lockout occurred due to multiple failed login attempts.
Support agents verified user identity before reactivating accounts.

Payment and Billing Issues:
Customers reported payment failures during checkout.
Issues were often caused by expired cards or bank transaction limits.
Agents advised customers to retry payment using a different method.
Refund-related queries were resolved within 5–7 business days.

Account Suspension Cases:
Temporary account suspension occurred due to policy violations.
After reviewing user activity, some accounts were reinstated with warnings.
Repeated violations resulted in permanent suspension.

Feature Usage Queries:
Customers frequently asked how to use newly released features.
Agents guided users through step-by-step instructions.
Reference links and screenshots were shared when necessary.

Service Downtime Complaints:
During maintenance windows, users experienced temporary outages.
Agents informed users about scheduled maintenance timelines.
Follow-up messages were sent after services were restored.

This knowledge base improves response consistency and reduces handling time.
