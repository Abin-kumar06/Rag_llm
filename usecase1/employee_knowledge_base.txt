Internal Employee Knowledge Base – Official Company Reference Document

This document serves as the centralized internal knowledge base for all employees.
It contains policies, procedures, guidelines, and best practices approved by the organization.

Remote Work Policy (2026):
The company supports a hybrid work culture to promote productivity and work-life balance.
Eligible employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote work arrangements may be granted for specific roles requiring minimal on-site presence.
Employees working remotely must remain available during core working hours from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST.
Remote employees must ensure a stable internet connection and a secure working environment.

Attendance and Work Hours:
Employees are expected to log attendance through the internal attendance portal.
Late logins beyond 15 minutes must be reported to the reporting manager.
Flexible work hours may be approved for employees with valid personal or medical reasons.

Leave Policy:
The organization offers annual leave, sick leave, casual leave, and compensatory leave.
All leave requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least two working days in advance.
Emergency leave may be applied retroactively with appropriate justification.

IT and Security Guidelines:
Employees must not share login credentials with anyone.
Company-issued devices should only be used for official purposes.
Any suspected security breach must be reported immediately to the IT helpdesk.

Code of Conduct:
Employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times.
Harassment, discrimination, or unethical conduct will lead to disciplinary action.

This document is reviewed annually and employees are advised to refer only to the latest version.
